We're Here to Help You Track

At PetrolTrack App, we believe managing vehicle expenses shouldn't be complicated. We empower drivers, families, and businesses to gain crystal-clear control over their fuel spending.

Never wonder where or when you filled up. PetrolTrack automatically logs the location, time, amount, and price per liter for every fuel purchase you record, tied directly to the correct vehicle.

Go beyond simple tracking. Our intuitive analysis tools instantly show you which months incurred the highest fuel costs, helping you budget better and identify spending patterns.

Stay informed. We provide the latest prevailing petrol price in your region, so you always know if you're getting a fair deal.

PetrolTrack is designed for all those who want to track their petrol expenses. Start tracking smarter today!
